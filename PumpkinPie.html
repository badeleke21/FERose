How to make Pumpkin Pie

What is your favorite thanksgiving dessert?

Ingredients

  • 1 premade pie crust
  • 1 can of pureed pumpkin
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 1/2 tsp of cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp of ginger
  • 1/4 tsp of cloves
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1 can of evaporated milk

Preheat

Preheat oven to 425 degrees

Prepare Filling

In a large bowl mix together the pumpkin puree, sugar, salt, cinnamon, ginger, and cloves. Slowly beat the eggs into the mixture one egg at time. After this egg is beaten in with the pumpkin and spice mixture, slowly add in the evaporatd milk. Mix all of this together until the mix is smooth and lump free.

Fill the crust

After unwrapping the crust, pour your pumpkin mixture into your premade crust.

Bake the pie

The oven should be 425 degrees by now, bake the pie in the oven at 425 for 15 minutes. Reduce this heat to 350 degrees and continue baking the rest of the pie for 40-50 minutes. You will know your pie is done when you insert a knife in the middle and the blade comes out clean.

Cool and serve

Allow the pie to cool completely before serving. This dish is best served with a hearty dallop of cool whip or whip cream.

In Conclusion

Hands down my favorite thanksgiving desert! This dessert is sure to be a hit at your next thanksgiving family dinner.
